--- notes: | An address lookup (sometimes called a ‘postcode lookup’) lets users specify a UK address by entering their postcode (and optionally their street name or number) and selecting their address from a list. **Advantages of address lookup** Using an address lookup means that: - people entering UK addresses don’t have to enter as much information manually - there’s less chance of mis-typed UK addresses **If you use an address lookup** When using an address lookup you should: - make it clear that the address lookup only works for UK addresses - provide a manual option for people with international addresses or addresses that are missing or badly formed in the Royal Mail database ---
