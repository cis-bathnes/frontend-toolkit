Bath Dial-a-Ride

Dial-a-Ride is a door-to-door, fully accessible minibus service for those who cannot use conventional public transport. Covering 3 areas within Bath and North East Somerset, you can use this service to go shopping, attend appointments and play an active part in community life. If you live within the 3 areas served by Dial-a-Ride and the Hopper service, and you cannot use conventional public transport, you should be eligible to register for this service.

For further information, please contact the co-ordinator of the relevant service. Please note that:

  • we operate on a first come first served basis
  • we may ask you to alter your desired journey times slightly
  • we reserve the right to refuse or defer an application for registration, to ask for additional information in support of an application or to cancel any existing registration
  • all wheelchairs must be risk assessed by us before we can transport you

Registration

You must register before you can book any journeys.

 

To register, please print and complete the registration form and return it to the address on the form or contact the co-ordinator on 01225 335 019.

Area covered

This service covers the area within the Bath city boundaries. There is a map showing the full extent of the service area in the information leaflet.

Service times

There are 2 buses working constantly within the service hours. The service operates between 9.30am and 2.30pm from Monday to Friday, excluding bank holidays.

Cost

Return journey: £6.00

Single journey: £3.00

Diamond Travelcard holders are entitled to a 50% discount.

Booking

To book a journey, call the co-ordinator on 01225 335 019.

Journeys can be booked a maximum of 7 days in advance.

All bookings must be made by midday on the day before the journey.

Don’t forget to book your return journey, if needed.

Please be ready 10 minutes before your journey time.
