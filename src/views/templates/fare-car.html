Fare Car

Fare Car is a public transport service providing a safe and friendly shared taxi service to and from Bath city centre at subsidised rates to everyone who lives in the following areas:

  • Langridge & Northend
  • Combe Hay, Shoscombe, South Stoke & Wellow

Registration

If this is the first time you’re using Fare Car, you need to register for your free Fare Car membership number. Just call 01225 477615 and give your name, address and telephone number. 

Booking

You need to book in advance. For journeys into Bath, please book at least 90 minutes before the scheduled time of arrival in Bath. For journeys back from Bath, please book at least 30 minutes before the time of departure. 

Before you book, you’ll need:

  • Fare Car membership number for each person wishing to travel
  • Day/date and time you wish to travel
  • Pick up location
  • Drop off location
  • Details of the homeward journey, if required

To make a booking, phone Bath Taxis on 01225 447777 between 8am and 4pm Monday to Friday. Note there is a free phone direct to Bath Taxis in the Marks & Spencer Foodhall.

Remember to mention if you’re a wheelchair user.

Cost

The fare is £1.50 per person per single trip. This is a subsidised fare and there are no further reductions for Diamond Travelcard holders or children.
