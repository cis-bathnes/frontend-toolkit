You’re entitled to a free bus pass if you’re a BANES resident and have reached the qualifying age or have certain disabilities. The bus pass is called a Diamond Travelcard.

How to use your bus pass
You must show your pass to the driver every time you get on a bus. Some bus companies will issue you with a ticket. If so, please keep the ticket until you get off the bus as you may need to show it to an inspector. If you forget to take your pass with you, you must pay the normal fare.

What you need to know

  • If you allow someone else to use your bus pass, it could be withdrawn
  • Your bus pass gives you no more rights than any other passenger
  • If you move within the council area, tell us your new address as soon as possible
  • If you move out of the council area, you’ll need to apply to your new council for a replacement pass
  • If you no longer require a pass, please return it to a council One Stop Shop
  • If you are applying for several passes on behalf of a group or organisation, you’ll need to make an appointment in advance by emailing triage@bathnes.gov.uk
