{{Title}}

You are eligible for a bus pass if

  • You have both arms but are unable to use them to carry out day to day tasks
  • You have a limb reduction deficiency of both arms
  • You have a bilateral upper limb amputation
  • You have a medical condition that affects the use of your arms such as motor neurone disease, muscular dystrophy, or spinal cord injury

You can take your documents into one of our One Stop Shops and we will process your application whilst you wait.
