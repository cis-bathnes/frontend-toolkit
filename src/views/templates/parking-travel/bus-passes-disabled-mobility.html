{{Title}}

YYou are eligible for a bus pass. You will need to provide evidence from a medical specialist which must show one of these statements

  • I’ve decided that you can stand and move unaided more than 20 metres but no more than 50 metres
  • I’ve decided that you can stand and then move using an aid or appliance more than 20 metres but not more than 50 metres
  • I’ve decided that you can stand and move more than 1 metre but no more than 20 metres
  • I’ve decided that you cannot stand or move more than 1 metre
  • You would be eligible for Higher Rate Mobility component of Disability Living Allowance (DLA)

You can take your documents into one of our One Stop Shops and we will process your application whilst you wait.
