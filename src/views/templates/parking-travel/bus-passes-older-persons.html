Apply for an older person’s bus pass

Check your eligibility

To get an older person’s bus pass (Diamond Travelcard), you must be a Bath and North East Somerset resident and of qualifying age.

Check your eligibility

How to get your bus pass

To get your bus pass, bring your proof of eligibility to the One Stop Shop in Bath, Keynsham or Midsomer Norton. We’ll check your details, take your photo free of charge and then print your card while you wait.

What you must bring

Proof of residence in Bath and North East Somerset

Please bring one of these (no more than 6 months old):

  • a council tax bill
  • a utility bill
  • a copy of your tenancy agreement
  • a bank statement
  • evidence of your entitlement to vote in Bath and North East Somerset
For boat dwellers only

Please bring one of these:

  • a mooring licence for a site in Bath and North East Somerset
  • evidence of your entitlement to vote in Bath and North East Somerset
  • a letter from a government department or public body addressed to you at a PO Box or Poste Restante in Bath and North East Somerset

Proof of identity

Please bring one of these:

  • birth certificate or adoption certificate
  • marriage or divorce certificate
  • passport
  • civil partnership or dissolution certificate
  • valid photo driving licence (both parts)

If you need a companion to assist you when travelling

If you need assistance to travel on buses, please bring confirmation from a healthcare professional when you apply. We can then endorse your bus pass to allow a companion to accompany you free on local buses. Companion entitlement is only valid in Bath and North East Somerset, Bristol, North Somerset, South Gloucestershire and Wiltshire.
