{{Title}}

If your business premises is in a residents parking zone, you may be entitled to up to 2 parking permits for essential business vehicles such as delivery vans.

Eligibility

You cannot apply for a business parking permit if

  • it's for a personal vehicle that you use for travelling to work
  • your premesis has any off-street parking
  • you are located in the Bath central zone
  • your building was constructed or converted after 2006

Mixing with other types of parking permit

These permits, together with business customer permits and any residents parking permits count towards your total maximum of 2 permits for your premesis. So for example you could apply for 1 essential vehicle and 1 customer permit, but not 2 of each.

How to apply

If necessary, check which parking zone you are in. Please note that business parking permits are not available in Bath central zone.

Apply online

How to use

A permit will allow you to park your business vehicle on the street within your allocated zone. The permit does not allow you to park in other zones and you can't park in time-limited waiting or pay-and-display only parking areas.

Already have a permit?

Most permits can be updated, renewed or cancelled online. This takes a couple of minutes and takes effect immediately.

Log in to manage your permits

Changing your vehicle

If you change your business vehicle you must update its details immediately to avoid getting a parking fine.

Renewing a permit

You can renew your permit up to 1 month before the expiry date. Renewal can be done online and only takes a couple of minutes. If you are renewing an existing permit all you need is your payment details. You must renew before the expiry date or you may be fined for parking in the zone.
