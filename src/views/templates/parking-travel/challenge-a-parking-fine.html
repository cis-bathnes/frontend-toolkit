You need to challenge a parking fine in writing within 28 days. You can do this in several ways.

Challenge a parking fine online

By email - parking@bathnes.gov.uk

You will need to quote the number from your fine, which will start with BN. You will also need to clearly state the reasons why you want to challenge the fine. If you need help with this, you can call 01225 477133 01225 477133 or visit a One Stop Shop.

If your challenge is rejected

If your challenge is rejected, you can make a formal representation. If you wish to do this then do not pay the fine. You will receive a letter 28 days after the fine was issued and this will tell you how you can make a representation.

If your representation is rejected

If your representation is rejected, you will be notified of this by letter. This letter will also tell you how you can appeal this decision. The appeal will be dealt with by the Traffic Penalty Tribunal and their decision will be final.

If you want independent advice you please contact PATROL UK
