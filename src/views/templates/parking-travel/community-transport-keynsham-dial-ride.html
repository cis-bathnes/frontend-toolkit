Keynsham and District Dial-a-Ride

{{Title}}

Registration

You must register before you can book any journeys.

 

To register, please print and complete the registration form  and return it to the address on the form. Alternatively, contact the co-ordinator on 01225 395 321.

Area covered

The following locations are covered:

  • Keynsham
  • Saltford
  • Whitchurch Village
  • Pensford
  • Publow
  • Compton Dando
  • Chewton Keynsham
  • Marksbury
  • Farmborough
  • Timsbury
  • Burnett
  • Newton St Loe
  • Corston

Further details can be found in the information leaflet.

Service times

Keynsham: 9am to 4pm from Monday to Friday.

Village service: 9am to 4.45pm from Monday to Thursday and 9am to 4.30pm on Fridays, excluding bank holidays.

All journeys and times are subject to demand.

Cost

Keynsham and Saltford: £4.00 return

Village service: £5.00 return

Diamond Travelcard holders are entitled to a 50% discount.

Booking

Keynsham and Saltford: Call 01225 395 321 between 9.30am to 11am on the day before transport is required.
