There are currently no secure coach parking facilities in Bath City Centre. All coaches must drop off and pick up passengers at Riverside Coach Park.

Secure coach parking sites

After dropping off all passengers at Riverside Coach Park, drivers can use one of the following secure coach parking sites:

  • Weston Island
  • Odd Down Coach Park

Bath Christmas Market Coach Parking

During Bath Christmas Market days in December there is a charge to park a coach and early booking is recommended to secure a place.
