Bath currently has no secure coach parking facilities within the City Centre. All coaches must drop off and pick up passengers at Riverside Coach Park.

Secure coach parking sites (Drivers Only)

Coach drivers can use one of the secure coach parking site below after all passengers have been dropped off.

  • Weston Island
  • Odd Down Park and Ride

Bath Christmas Market Coach Parking

During the Bath Christmas Market coach parking facilites change slightly due to the demand during this busy period
