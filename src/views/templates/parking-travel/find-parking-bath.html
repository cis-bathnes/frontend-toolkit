Disabled parking

All car parks except Cattle Market and Claverton Street have disabled spaces.

Parking for taller vehicles

Height restrictions are shown in the detailed listing for each car park. Charlotte Street car park does not have a height restriction and is recommended for vans, motorhomes, campervans and other tall vehicles.

On-street parking

A number of streets in central Bath have paid-for, on-street parking.

See on-street parking on a map

On-street parking charges

  • On-street parking is normally more expensive than using a car park in a similar location, please check the signs at the roadside for details.
  • You can pay by phone at all on-street pay and display locations
  • Pay and display machines accept coins (but not notes)
  • Charges apply Monday–Saturday 8am–7pm. At other times, and on Bank Holidays, you can park here for free.

If you are a Blue Badge holder, view details of where you can use your badge and any charges that may apply.

Motorcycles

There is no charge for motorcyclists at these on-street locations. However, maximum stay restrictions also apply and motorcycles are required to be parked within designated bays.
