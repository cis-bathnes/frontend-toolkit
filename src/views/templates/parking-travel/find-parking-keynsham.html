Car parks

See all Keynsham car parks on a map

There are 8 council-run car parks in Keynsham:

  • Ashton Way
  • Ashton Way East
  • Bath Hill East
  • Civic Centre
  • Fox and Hounds
  • The Labbott South
  • The Labbott North
  • Station Road

On-street parking

There is no paid-for on-street parking in Keynsham. You can park on the road where it is safe to do so, but you must observe any restrictions marked including yellow lines.
