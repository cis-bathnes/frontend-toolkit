Why can't I get a permit?

This property is not entitled to any parking permits or visitor permits because it was built or converted after 2006. Parking in Bath is hugely over-crowded and unfortunately we have had to stop permits being issued for new properties. Find out more about this in the Transport and parking policy.

What are my options?

  • For short-term use you may want to look at nearby car parks and on-street parking. Residents apply for a 10% discount on car parking charges.
  • You might also want to consider joining a city car sharing club
  • Bath also has extensive public transport links
  • If you do not agree with this entitlement ruling you have the right to appeal.

Motorcycles

Please note that you do not need a permit to park a motorcycle on the street
