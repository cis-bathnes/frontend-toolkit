  • £10 for 100 hours (maximum 1000 hours per year) bought online

How to buy

Residents parking and visitor parking permits can be bought online. You'll need your council tax bill reference in order to show that you live at this address. Applying for a permit online usually takes around 10 minutes and your permit will be activated immediately.

Buy residents or visitor permits online

Paper visitor permits

If you'd prefer to use paper scratch card visitor permits, you can buy these in-person from a One-stop shop. If your name is not on the Council Tax accout where you live then please take a signed, current copy of your tenancy agreement with you.

Please note

  • You must live at this address at least 4 days per week to qualify for a permit
  • Permits for this address also qualify you to park in Charlotte Street car park
  • Your permit will only allow you to park in zone 3
  • Your permit will not allow you to park in a pay & display only or in time-limited waiting parking bays.
  • Motorcycles do not require a permit to park in the zone.

For full permit rules please see the terms & conditions.
