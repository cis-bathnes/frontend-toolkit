Avoid your guests having problems when parking near your holiday let in a residents parking zone by registering for a holiday let parking permit.

Permits allow you to:

  • Park in residents parking bays or paid parking bays in the residents parking zone your holiday let is located in

How much do they cost?

Holiday let permits cost Â£100 each

Who can apply?

Any proprietor of any holiday let within a parking zone. The proprietor must pay Business Rates on the property and the property must be available for let for over 140 days of the year.

You will need to provide

  • Evidence that the holiday let is registered
  • A copy of a current business rates demand
Apply now
It can take up to 10 days for your application to progress.

Using your permit

The paper permit will need to be clearly displayed in the windscreen of any vehicle used by guests that are parked in a residents parking zone. The permit should be clearly filled out and show that it has been issued to your hotel/guest house.

Failure to display a valid permit may result in a parking ticket being issued to your guests.
