{{Title}}

Avoid your guests having problems when parking near your hotel or guest house in a residents parking zone by registering for a hotel/guest house parking permit.

Permits allow you to:

  • Park on street in the residents parking zone your hotel/guest house is located in
Charges
1 - 5 permits £80 each
6 - 10 permits £105 each
11 - 15 £160 each

You are only allowed to charge your guests the equivalent of the daily cost of the permit for each day of use. Any guest houses/hotels charging guests more than this may have their permits revoked.

Who can apply?

Any registered hotel/guest house within a parking zone.

You will need to provide

  • Evidence that the hotel/guest house is registered
  • A copy of a current national non-domestic council tax demand
Apply now
It can take up to 10 days for your application to progress.

Using your permit

The paper permit will need to be clearly displayed in the windscreen of any vehicle used by guests that are parked in a residents parking zone. The permit should be clearly filled out and show that it has been issued to your hotel/guest house.

Failure to display a valid permit may result in a parking ticket being issued to your guests.
