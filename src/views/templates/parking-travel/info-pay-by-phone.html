Smartphone app

Download the MiPermit Android app or Apple iOS app to your phone (you might want to do this before you arrive, to ensure you have a good network connection). You'll need to create an account and register your vehicle before you can make your first payment.

  

Do you live locally?

Local residents can get a 10% discount (you must register and apply for this before paying for your parking).

Text message

New customers

Send an SMS to 61600 with the word PARK and your vehicle registration number. For example:

PARK GY19GGK

Our automated system will call you back for the following information.

  • Car park location number
  • Your payment card details
  • Whether you want to receive an SMS reminder 20 minutes before the stay expires

Already registered?

Send an SMS text message to 61600 with the word PARK and the location number and duration in hours or minutes. For example:

PARK 702233 30mins

PARK 702233 90mins

PARK 702233 2hrs

PARK 702233 1day

If you want to add a new vehicle to your account, or have more than one vehicle on your account already, you can stipulate which vehicle to use by adding the registration number to the end of the text message. For example:

PARK 702233 30mins GY19GGK

Please note that once you have more than one vehicle on your account, you will need to add the registration number of the vehicle you wish to park to all messages. If you do not, the system will take the last vehicle added to your account as default.

Extending your stay

To extend your stay, send a message to 61600 with the word EXTEND and the duration to extend by. For example:

EXTEND 30mins

You won't be able to extend a stay past its opening hours, or if the location has a maximum stay limit.
