Avoid problems when parking when visiting patients under your care by registering for a medical parking permit before visiting residents parking zones.

Permits allow you to:

  • Park in any residents parking zone for as long as you need
  • Park in residents parking bays or paid parking bays

This permit cannot be used for staff parking, and cannot be used for parking at your surgery or practice.

Who can apply?

GPs who need to park in a residents parking zone to visit a patient under their care. You do not need to be based locally.

Applications can be made by a Practice Manager or similar role

Medical professionals might include

  • Doctors
  • District Nurses
  • Community Health Nurses
  • Midwives
  • Home Care Assistants
  • Health Visitors

Applications can be made by a Practice Manager or similar role.

You'll need to provide:

  • Evidence that the named person is a qualified medical professional

If applying as an individual you will also need to provide a supporting letter from your practice, surgery, or health trust.

Apply now
It can take up to 10 days for your application to progress.

    Activate online

    Log-in using your mobile number and PIN, go to the Trade Parking page and select your vehicle, location and duration required. Save and confirm your selection to finish.


    Alternative ways to activate your stay

    E.g. TRADE 755998 2hours
    to 60300

    Activate by telephone

    Call the automated system on 0333 123 9009 from your registered mobile number.

    Input your zone code, vehicle and duration of stay.
