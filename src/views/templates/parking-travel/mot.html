Services

We offer a range of services to individuals and taxi/minicab drivers using our state-of-the-art testing equipment and workshop facilities:

  • MOT testing
  • Servicing, Diagnostics, Repairs
  • Licensing inspection (taxi/minicab)
  • Meter testing (taxi/minicab)
  • Brake testing (vehicles over 3.5T)
  • Minibus driver training

Brake test

In addition to its compulsory annual test, it is recommended that you get your commercial or PCV vehicle brakes tested at least three times per year. We offer rolling road brake testing services for vehicles over 3.5T at a cost of £10 (+VAT) per axle.

Please note we only accept payments by debit or credit card.
