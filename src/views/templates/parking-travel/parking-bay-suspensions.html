If you are moving home, expecting a delivery, or need access to a set of parking bays for another reason, such as tree pruning or building works, you can apply for a suspension of parking bay suspension.

If you want to deposit a skip on any road, you will have to buy a Skip licence .

How to Apply

  • Download the application form (37KB, MS Word)
  • Complete the form
  • Email it to parking@bathnes.gov.uk

Charges

Prices are per standard parking space. For long wheelbase vehicles or lorries two or more spaces would be required.

Amount
Single or first space All zones/areas £44
Additional spaces Central & Zone £29
All other zones/areas £10
