To pay for a parking fine you will need the following:

  • The Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) Number (found on your parking ticket)
  • The vehicle registration number
Pay your parking penalty charge notice

Other ways to pay your parking penalty charge notice

By phone

Please call Call 01225 477133 to pay over the phone with a debit or credit card (office hours only).

In person at Council Offices

Payments can be made at One Stop Shops in Bath, Keynsham and Midsomer Norton.

Self-service payment kiosks are available for cash, debit and credit card payments. These machines are unable to accept Â£50 notes.

You'll need to have your reference number with you.

Automatic receipts are provided for payments.

Pay by post

Please send a cheque payable to Parking Services, Bath and North East Somerset Council, PO Box 5197, Bath, BA1 0UF. Clearly write your name, address and notice number on the back of your cheque.

We cannot accept post-dated cheques and we do not send receipts for cheque payments.
