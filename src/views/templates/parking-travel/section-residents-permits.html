Changing your car

If you get a different car you must update your vehicle details immediately to avoid getting a parking fine.

Renewing a permit

You can renew your permit up to 1 month before the expiry date. Renewal can be done online and only takes a couple of minutes. If you are renewing an existing permit all you need is your payment details. You must renew before the expiry date or you may be fined for parking in the zone.

Moving house, transferring and cancelling permits

You must cancel your parking permit as soon as you move out. You can do this online by logging into your account. Please note that we don't offer refunds on the time remaining on your permit. However, if you are moving to another property in Bath & North East Somerset, and that property is also entitled to a parking permit then the balance of your permit can be transferred. How to use

You can park on the street within your allocated zone. Your permit does not allow you to park in other zones and you can't park in time-limited waiting or pay-and-display only parking areas. You must also obey all other parking regulations such as yellow lines.

How to use visitor permits

For electronic visitor permits, use the MiPermit app to enter the vehicle registration, then select a duration to allocate the permit. You can also do this by text, website or phone.

For paper permits, scratch off the date for which you intend to use the permit for and fill in your visitor's vehicle registration number. Ask them to display this on the dashboard of the vehicle.

Report illegal parking

If you suspect someone is parking in a residents zone illegally you can let us know and we'll look into it. Due to data privacy laws we won't be able to confirm to you whether someone else's car has a valid permit, but if we find an issue then we will take action.
