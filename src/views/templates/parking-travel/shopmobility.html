Shopmobility is a loan scheme that provides mobility vehicles to help people get around in Bath city centre. If you have limited mobility (even temporarily), the service is available to you regardless of your age or disability.

What’s available

The following mobility vehicles are available for hire:

  • Powered wheelchairs
  • Manual wheelchairs
  • Electric scooters

Booking

Ways to book:

  • Visit the One Stop Shop at 3 to 4 Manvers Street, Bath BA1 1JQ (View on local map)
  • Call 01225 481 744
  • Email shop_mobility@bathnes.gov.uk

We recommend that you book in advance. Please bring proof of identity when collecting your vehicle. We’ll show you how to operate the vehicle safely and you’ll have a chance to practice before taking it out on your own.

Collection

Please collect your vehicle from the One Stop Shop, 3 to 4 Manvers Street, Bath BA1 1JQ.

Cost

There’s a small hire charge to cover running costs.

Electric scooter

£1.00 + VAT per hour (or part of)

Powered wheelchair

£1.00 + VAT per hour (or part of)

Manual wheelchair

50p + VAT per hour (or part of)

Overnight hire

£3.50 + VAT for overnight hire
(overnight charges are payable in advance and are non-refundable)

All charges are subject to 20% VAT. You may be exempt from paying VAT if you’re chronically sick or disabled. For more information, please call Shopmobility or ask at the One Stop Shop.
