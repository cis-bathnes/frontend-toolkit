Avoid parking problems your van by registering for a trade parking permit in advance of visiting residents parking zones.

Permits allow you to:

  • Park in any residents parking zone for as long as you need
  • Park in residents parking bays or paid parking bays
  • Register (+ use???) multiple vehicles for your business
Charges
Zone Charge
Central zone and Zone 1 £3 per hour
All other zones £6.60 per day

Who can apply?

Any tradesperson who needs to continuously park in a resdients parking zone to access goods and materials. You do not need to be based locally.

Apply now
It can take up to 10 days for your application to progress. You will need to provide an image of your letterhead or similar to prove your business is legitimate.

Using your permit
  1. Check your zone
    or use the following zone codes:
    755999 = Central and zone 1
    755998 = All other zones
  2. Activate your stay...

    The easiest way to activate your trade permit is to use the MiPermit smartphone app

    Download for iPhone Download for Android

    Alternatives...

    Activate online

    Activate online
    Log-in using your mobile number and PIN, go to the Trade Parking page and select your vehicle, location and duration required. Save and confirm your selection to finish.

    Activate by SMS

    Text: "TRADE" + zone code + duration of stay
    E.g. TRADE 755998 2hours

    to 60300

    Activate by telephone

    Call the automated system on 0333 123 9009 from your registered mobile number.
    Input your zone code, vehicle and duration of stay.
  3. Problem? Call 0333 123 8008
