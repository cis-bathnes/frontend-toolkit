In order to pay your Council invoice online you will need to know your reference number. This can be found on the top right hand side of your bill.

Other ways to pay your Council invoice

By phone

You can pay your allotment bill by calling the automated payments line on 0845 6035058, available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Payments can only be made by all major credit and debit cards.

In person at Council Offices

Payments can be made at One Stop Shops in Bath, Keynsham and Midsomer Norton.

You'll need to have your account / reference / invoice number with you.

Automatic receipts are provided for payments.
